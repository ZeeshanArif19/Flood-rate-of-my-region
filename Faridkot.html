What to Do During a Flood


1.Follow Evacuation Orders

If authorities issue evacuation orders, promptly follow them to ensure your safety and that of your family.

2.Move to Higher Ground

Seek higher ground away from flood-prone areas, preferably a sturdy building on elevated terrain, to minimize the risk of being submerged.

3.Avoid Walking or Driving Through Flooded Areas

Never attempt to walk or drive through flooded areas, as water currents can be deceptively strong and road conditions may be compromised.

4.Listen to Emergency Alerts

Pay attention to emergency alerts and instructions from local authorities to stay updated on the evolving situation and take necessary precautions.

5.Stay Away from Power Lines and Electrical Equipment

Avoid contact with power lines and electrical equipment, as they could be energized or pose other hazards during flooding.
